Permanent Solutions

Dental Implants

The gold standard for missing teeth. A titanium implant replaces the root, and a custom crown sits on top — giving you a tooth that looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural one. Placed with 3D-guided surgical precision.

3D-Guided Precision

How It Works

  • 3D intraoral scan maps your jaw before any procedure — millimetre-perfect planning
  • Surgical guide manufactured from your scan for pinpoint implant placement
  • Titanium implant placed under local anaesthesia — minimal trauma, fast recovery
  • Instant digital X-ray confirms perfect positioning immediately after placement
  • Custom zirconia crown matched to your natural tooth colour and shape
  • Lasts a lifetime with normal care — no decay, no movement, no discomfort

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Quick Dental Care located?+

We are in Akhalia, Sylhet — near Mount Adora Hospital. Easily reached by CNG, rickshaw, or private car from all parts of Sylhet city.

What are your opening hours?+

Saturday–Thursday: 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Friday: 4:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Emergency WhatsApp is available 24/7 at +88 01307978439.

Do you offer flexible payment plans?+

Yes — we offer instalment-based plans for most major treatments including implants, braces, aligners, and prosthodontic work. Speak to our team when booking to discuss your options.

What is a 3D intraoral scan and does it hurt?+

A small wand scans inside your mouth and creates a precise 3D digital model in under 3 minutes — no putty, no discomfort at all. It is used for implant planning, crown design, and aligner fabrication.

Are dental implants painful?+

The procedure is done under local anaesthesia — you will not feel pain during it. Some mild soreness for 2–3 days afterward is normal. Our 3D-guided technique minimises tissue trauma for faster, more comfortable healing.

Clear aligners or braces — which should I choose?+

For mild to moderate crowding and spacing, clear aligners are an excellent discreet option. For complex bite issues, severe crowding, or jaw problems, braces are generally more effective. Our orthodontist will recommend the right option after a consultation.

Is digital X-ray safe?+

Yes — our digital X-ray uses up to 90% less radiation than conventional film X-rays. Results appear on screen in seconds for faster, more accurate diagnosis. Very safe for routine use, including for children.
